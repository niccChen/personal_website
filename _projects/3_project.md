**Aug 2025 – Present**

Proposed EPI-SSM, a probabilistic sequence model for multi-horizon epidemic forecasting integrating patch-based temporal representations, latent state rollout, and heteroscedastic Gaussian emissions.

- Built an end-to-end pipeline over regional surveillance time series with temporal data leakage constraints and zero-shot transfer evaluation
- Implemented variance-regularized likelihood objectives to mitigate over-dispersed uncertainty estimates
- Evaluated with CRPS, calibration score, and coverage-sensitive metrics
- Ran large-scale baseline comparisons across RNN, CNN, linear, Transformer, and epidemiology-specific models

**Tools:** Python, PyTorch, WHO COVID-19 data, Tycho dataset

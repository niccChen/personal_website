I am an undergraduate student at **Emory University** pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics & Statistics (GPA: 4.0, Dean's List). My work sits at the intersection of **machine learning, NLP, and applied AI**, with a focus on building systems that are both technically rigorous and practically grounded.

**Research.** I currently work on three projects: (1) weakly supervised clinical inconsistency detection in longitudinal EHR notes using MIMIC-IV, achieving 0.936 macro-F1; (2) a counterfactual benchmark for evaluating multimodal LLM robustness to misleading chart encodings, testing GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, and Gemini 2.0 Flash; and (3) EPI-SSM, a probabilistic sequence model for multi-horizon epidemic forecasting with heteroscedastic uncertainty estimation.

**Industry.** I have industry experience as an AI/LLM engineer, having built RAG pipelines and knowledge agents at Emory's Office of Undergraduate Admission, engineered document intelligence systems at Outamation, and prototyped MCP-based internal assistants at the Postal Savings Bank of China.

I am broadly interested in **clinical NLP, multimodal reasoning, and probabilistic forecasting**. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to chat about research or collaboration.
